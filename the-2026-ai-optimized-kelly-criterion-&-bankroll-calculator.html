Understanding the Kelly Criterion in 2026

The Kelly Criterion is a mathematical formula designed to maximize the long-term growth of your bankroll. The classic formula is:

f* = (bp - q) / b

  • f* is the fraction of the bankroll to wager.
  • b is the decimal odds minus 1.
  • p is the probability of winning.
  • q is the probability of losing (1 - p).
