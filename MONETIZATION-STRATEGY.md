# DailyAI Betting - Monetization Strategy & Research

## Market Overview

The global online sports betting market hit **$44.2 billion in 2024** with continued rapid growth as more US states legalize. This is a massive opportunity for affiliate sites.

**Key 2025 Trends:**
- 70%+ of betting traffic is mobile
- SEO/content sites outperforming paid traffic due to ad restrictions
- RevShare with bonuses gaining momentum over pure CPA
- AI-powered tools for personalization becoming standard
